Welcome at Belintertrans-Germany GmbH

"BELINTERTRANS - Transport and Logistic Centre" a national company of the Belarus railway (BTLC) has founded the Belintertrans-Germany GmbH in September 2012 in Germany.

The BLTC offers all kinds of forwarding services from the regions of Belarus and the CIS-States up to China.

The foundation of Belintertrans-Germany GmbH enables European clients direct purchase of freights and services in the sector of logistics. In order to keep up to the standards of European service we perceive short and efficient communication as one of our top priorities. From this moment on, professional, experienced and multilingual employees are at your service to provide a fast, safe and trouble-free delivery of your goods.

With structurised and strategically important subsidiaries in Brest, Klaipeda, Minsk and Moscow, Belintertrans is capable of providing following services for you:

  • Container transports (Sea containers, Tank containers etc.) from the EU-CIS-China on purchase and rental basis
  • Wagon and heavy transports on purchase and rental basis
  • Trucking with our own fleet
  • Project logistics

We look forward to working with you.
